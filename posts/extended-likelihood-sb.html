Extended Likelihood for Signal vs Background

Extended likelihood incorporates both the shape information and the total expected count. For a Poisson count \(\nu(\theta)\) and per‑event PDF \(f(x\mid\theta)\), the extended likelihood is

$$ \mathcal{L}(\theta) = e^{-\nu(\theta)} \prod_{i=1}^n \nu(\theta) f(x_i\mid\theta). $$

This is common in signal vs background fits in HEP.
