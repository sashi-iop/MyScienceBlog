Kolmogorov–Smirnov Test: A Gentle Guide

The Kolmogorov–Smirnov (K–S) test compares the empirical CDF of data against a reference CDF. It is useful for goodness‑of‑fit checks without binning.

The statistic is $$D = \sup_x | F_n(x) - F(x) |,$$ where \(F_n\) is the empirical CDF and \(F\) the model CDF.
